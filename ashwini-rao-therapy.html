You will be working with Ashwini

Ashwini Rao is the Founder of Pure Harmony and a counselling psychologist with 8+ years of experience working closely with Indian couples, individuals, and families.

She holds an M.Phil. in Psychology and specialises in marriage counselling, communication breakdowns, trust issues, emotional transitions, and self-esteem concerns.

Her approach is calm, non-judgemental, and practical. The focus is not on blame, but on understanding what is happening beneath the conflict and moving forward safely.

Languages: English · Tamil · Kannada · Tulu
