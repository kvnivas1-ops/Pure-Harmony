Why PHCFN Exists

Supporting the people who dedicate their lives to supporting others.

Most psychology graduates leave university with strong theoretical foundations but limited access to mentorship, peer consultation, professional referrals, and practical guidance for building a meaningful career.

The Pure Harmony Clinical Fellowship Network was created to bridge that gap. We bring together psychologists, counselors, therapists, and mental health professionals who are committed to ethical practice, continuous learning, and professional excellence.

PHCFN is not a certification course. It is a long-term professional ecosystem where practitioners can learn, collaborate, support one another, and grow together.

Our Philosophy

Collaboration builds stronger professionals and better client outcomes.

Mental health professionals often spend their careers helping others, yet many build those careers in isolation.

We believe growth happens faster when practitioners can learn together, seek support without judgment, exchange ideas openly, and contribute to a community larger than themselves.

PHCFN is built on the principles of ethical practice, generosity, continuous learning, and collective growth.

A Simple Belief

When psychologists support one another, clients receive better care.

When practitioners grow together, the profession grows stronger.

No therapist should have to build their journey alone.

Meet the Founder

Building a stronger ecosystem for mental health professionals.

Ashwini Rao

Ashwini Rao is a counseling psychologist, professor, researcher, published author, and founder of Pure Harmony Counseling.

With years of experience in therapy, marital counseling, teaching, supervision, and mental health research, she has worked closely with both clients and emerging professionals.

Through PHCFN, her vision is to create a thriving professional community where psychologists can continue learning, receive support, build meaningful relationships, and grow sustainable careers.
