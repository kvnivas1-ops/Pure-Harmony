Is therapy right for you right now?

People come to Pure Harmony with many questions. You might be:

  • Feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or low on most days
  • Going through repeated arguments or distance in your relationship
  • Balancing work, parenting, and your own mental health
  • Looking for a neutral, non-judgmental space to process things

You do not need to “have it all figured out” before starting therapy. That is what the space is for.

What we offer

Dedicated support for you as an individual, and for you as a couple.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on helping you understand your thoughts, emotions, and patterns, and build healthier ways of coping.

  • Stress, anxiety, and low mood
  • Self-esteem and confidence concerns
  • Work, academic, or career-related pressures
  • Family conflicts and life transitions

Suitable for adults and older adolescents. Online and in-person options available.

Couple Therapy

Joint sessions for partners who want to understand each other better, handle recurring conflicts, and strengthen their relationship.

  • Communication breakdown or frequent arguments
  • Pre-marital counseling and early-stage conflicts
  • Trust issues, distance, or emotional disconnection
  • Balancing couplehood, parenting, and extended family

Both partners are encouraged to attend. Sessions are structured, neutral, and solution-focused.

Frequently asked questions

How long is one session?

Most sessions are 50 to 60 minutes.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, online sessions can be arranged depending on the therapist’s schedule. You can mention your preference when you book.

Can I come alone first if it is a couple issue?

Yes. Many partners start with individual sessions to gain clarity before bringing their partner into couple sessions.
