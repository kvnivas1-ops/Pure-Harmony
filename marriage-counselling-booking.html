Feeling stuck in your marriage?

If your conversations keep turning into arguments or silence, couple therapy can give you a calm, structured way to talk things through with a professional by your side.

In your sessions with Ashwini Rao, counselling psychologist (8+ years experience, M.Phil.), you will:
  • Talk without constant fights or blame
  • Understand what is really hurting both of you
  • Learn simple ways to communicate your needs better
  • Get practical steps to bring back connection and respect

You don’t have to be “on the verge of divorce” to seek help. Many Indian couples come when they just know, deep down, that things cannot continue like this.

What you get from couple therapy

The focus is not on deciding who is right or wrong. The focus is on understanding, respect, and next steps.

A neutral space to talk
Both of you get equal space. The session is guided so it doesn’t turn into another fight at home.
Clarity about what’s really going wrong
Instead of arguing about small things, you understand the deeper patterns, hurts, and expectations on both sides.
Healthier communication
Learn how to share what you feel without attacking or shutting down, so your partner can actually hear you.
Support for Indian family realities
Sessions consider in-laws, children, money stress, work pressure, and cultural expectations that are common in Indian homes.
Small, realistic changes
You leave with simple, doable steps instead of heavy “homework” that you can’t follow in real life.
A plan for the way forward
Together with Ashwini, you decide what you want from this relationship and how to move towards it at a comfortable pace.

You will be working with Ashwini

Ashwini Rao is a counselling psychologist with 8+ years of experience working with Indian couples, individuals, and families.

She has an M.Phil. in Psychology and specialises in marriage counselling, relationship issues, communication breakdowns, trust concerns, postpartum emotional changes, and self-esteem difficulties.

Her style is warm, calm, and practical. The goal is not to judge either of you, but to help both partners slow down, understand what is happening underneath the conflicts, and rebuild connection at a pace that feels safe.

Languages: English · Tamil · Kannada · Tulu

All sessions are confidential and handled with care.
